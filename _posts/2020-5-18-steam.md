添加到购物车，锁购买的要转区才行：

```js
jQuery.post('//store.steampowered.com/checkout/addfreelicense',{action:'add_to_cart',sessionid:g_sessionID,subid:<subid>})
https://store.steampowered.com/sub/<subid>
javascript:addToCart(<subid>);
```
